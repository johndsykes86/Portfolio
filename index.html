Who am I?

I am a web developer working primarily in React, Express, Javascript, CSS and HTML as well as Ruby and Ruby on Rails.

Before I was a developer, I was a technical support analyst at an ERP software company. While there, I used my troubleshooting, documentation, and interpersonal skills to deliver excellent customer service and bridge the gap between what customers were saying and what they were actually looking for or trying to fix. This has helped me immensely as a budding developer; being able to debug code and turn sometimes vague feedback into something tangible* and actionable is something that I pride myself on.

Currently, I'm working on OneTwo, which is a Foursquare clone for checking into and sharing experiences at MLS (Major League Soccer) stadiums as well as learning Python.
